Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Ordering

  1. Browse our products and find the item you want
  2. Click on the product to view details
  3. Click the "Order via WhatsApp" button
  4. WhatsApp will open with a pre-filled message
  5. Review the message and send it to us
  6. Our team will respond shortly to confirm your order

Shipping

Shipping times vary by location:

  • Local (within 50 miles): 1-2 business days
  • Domestic: 3-7 business days
  • International: 7-14 business days

Please note that these are estimates and actual delivery times may vary.

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and order size:

  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 - $12.99
  • Express Shipping: $15.99 - $24.99
  • Free Shipping: On orders over $100

Exact shipping costs will be provided when you place your order.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us via WhatsApp for specific rates to your country.
Payment

We accept various payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer / Wire Transfer
  • Mobile Money (M-Pesa, etc.)
  • Cash on Delivery (selected areas)
  • PayPal (for international orders)

Payment details will be discussed during your WhatsApp conversation.

Currently, we process payments through WhatsApp conversations rather than direct online payments. This allows us to provide personalized service and discuss payment options that work best for you.

For orders over $500, we may be able to arrange payment plans. Please discuss this with our team via WhatsApp, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be unused and in original packaging
  • Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

Contact us via WhatsApp to initiate a return.

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp with your order number and reason for return
  2. We'll provide you with a return authorization and shipping address
  3. Package the item securely with original packaging
  4. Ship the item back to us (keep tracking information)
  5. Once received, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method. You'll receive a confirmation via WhatsApp once the refund is processed.
